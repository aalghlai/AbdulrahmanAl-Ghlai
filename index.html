About

Hi! My name is Abdulrahman Al-Ghlai, and welcome to my personal portfolio. I am a first-generation American of Libyan heritage, born in Egypt. In 2009, my family and I moved to the United States, where I grew up with my parents and now four younger brothers. I am currently pursuing a bachelor's in Statistics and Data Science at UC Berkeley, where my academic interests are focused on AI, data science, and statistical analysis.

In addition to my studies, I am passionate about both playing and watching a wide range of sports. I enjoy building and creating, whether it's through coding projects or hands-on work. My dedication to these fields reflects my love for problem-solving and continuous learning. I strive to apply my skills to real-world challenges, particularly in the areas of data, analytics, and innovative technologies!

Experience

Data Science Intern @ The Mango Jelly

At The Mango Jelly, I am part of a team developing AI-powered tools using Llama2 and Contextual Retrieval for Multimodal RAG to automate data extraction and enhance insights across diverse data types. My work has focused on optimizing data retrieval processes to improve system efficiency. I implemented PyMuPDF as an alternative to LlamaParse, achieving a 55% reduction in operational costs and boosting multimodal system performance with a 40% decrease in response time. Additionally, we are incorporating these tools into an AI chatbot for our website, designed to enhance user interaction and deliver personalized support. I also optimized the LlamaIndex pipelines for real-time data integration and analytics, fine-tuning models and applying prompt caching to increase retrieval accuracy and accelerate response times across platforms.

Software Engineer Intern @ DT Master Carbon

My work led to a reduction in load times, resulting in a faster and smoother user experience. I assisted in setting up development environments and prioritized customer-facing features, which streamlined payment processes and drove a 15% increase in user adoption. Additionally, I optimized APIs to enable efficient data exchange between the client and server, reducing latency and boosting system performance. Projects

NBA Player Salary Predictor

In Fall 2024, I created a predictive model using linear regression to estimate NBA player salaries based on key performance metrics, achieving an accuracy of 87% in salary predictions and supporting budget planning for teams. I utilized Pandas and NumPy for data manipulation and feature engineering, which enhanced the model’s robustness and improved prediction accuracy by incorporating essential player performance indicators. Using Matplotlib, I visualized the complex relationships between player statistics and projected salaries, providing analysts with actionable insights into how specific performance metrics can impact earnings potential.

Real Estate Housing Prediction Model

In Summer 2024, I built a linear regression model to accurately forecast housing prices, achieving 82% accuracy by comparing predicted outcomes with actual market values, which provided valuable insights for investors and buyers. I employed Matplotlib and Seaborn to visualize key factors like market trends, location, and square footage, enabling a deeper understanding of variables influencing housing prices. By analyzing the impact of various property attributes on pricing, I delivered actionable insights that equipped stakeholders with strategic knowledge for assessing property values across different market conditions.

Titanic Investigation Analysis

In Summer 2024, I worked on analyzing the Titanic dataset to figure out what factors influenced passenger survival. I cleaned up the data by handling missing values and created new features like age groups and family size to get a clearer picture. Using tools like Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, and Seaborn, I visualized survival rates based on things like gender, passenger class, and where people boarded the ship. These visualizations helped reveal some key insights into how different factors affected survival chances.
